Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Lexoral provides certified bilingual legal transcription services with the highest standards of confidentiality, security, and professional responsibility. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of all client information and legal materials entrusted to us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact and professional details (name, law firm, email, phone, billing information)
  • Audio recordings submitted for transcription
  • Case-related information and proceeding details
  • Transcripts, notes, and supporting documents
  • Communications and support requests

2. How We Use Your Information

All information is used exclusively for:

  • Providing accurate and timely legal transcription services
  • Quality assurance, verification, and formatting
  • Client communication and project management
  • Billing and administrative purposes
  • Complying with legal or regulatory obligations

3. Confidentiality & Professional Obligations

Client recordings, transcripts, and all related materials are treated as strictly confidential. We maintain internal policies and technical safeguards consistent with the professional standards expected in legal proceedings. Access to client files is strictly limited to personnel bound by confidentiality obligations.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures including:

  • Encrypted file transfers (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure storage with restricted access
  • Regular security reviews
  • Confidential workflow protocols

5. Artificial Intelligence & Model Training

Client data is never used to train any public or third-party AI models. Any transcription technology used is deployed solely for the purpose of fulfilling your specific service request and is subject to strict confidentiality controls.

6. Data Retention

We retain client files only as long as necessary to complete the requested services, meet quality and professional standards, and comply with legal or regulatory requirements. Upon request, we can permanently delete recordings and transcripts.

7. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You may at any time request:

  • Access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Permanent deletion of recordings and transcripts

To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@lexoral.org.

8. International Transfers

As a service provider operating internationally, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place when personal data is transferred across borders, in compliance with applicable data protection laws (including PIPEDA and Québec privacy legislation).

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The revised version will be posted with an updated “Last updated” date.
