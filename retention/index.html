Retention Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Purpose

Lexoral maintains temporary retention of files and transcripts for operational continuity, quality assurance, and client support purposes.

Retention Period

Unless otherwise agreed in writing, submitted recordings and transcripts may be retained for a limited operational period.

Deletion Requests

Clients may request permanent deletion of recordings, transcripts, and related files at any time.

Confidential Handling

All retained materials are handled through restricted-access confidentiality procedures.

Contact

For retention or deletion requests, contact:

contact@lexoral.org
